Data Deletion Request

Apple requires that users have the right to delete their accounts and all associated data. To delete your TracknMe account:

  1. Open the TracknMe app.
  2. Go to Settings > Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Alternatively, you can request manual data deletion by emailing us at support@yourdomain.com. Please include your registered email address.
