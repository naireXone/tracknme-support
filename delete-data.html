Delete Your Data

TracknMe respects your privacy. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

Method 1: Clear App Data

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Scroll down to Danger Zone
  3. Tap Wipe Current Sheet Data
  4. Tap Clear Data or Clear Storage

This will permanently delete all your data for that sheet

Method 2: Uninstall the App

Simply uninstalling TracknMe from your device will remove all locally stored data.

Method 3: Request Purchase Data Deletion

If you made a purchase and want your purchase history deleted from our payment processor, please contact us directly.
