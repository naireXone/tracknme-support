Delete Your Data

Last updated: January 2026

TracknMe respects your privacy. All your budget data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers. Because we do not store your financial data, you have full control over its deletion.

Method 1: Clear App Data (In-App)

To delete specific data within the app:

  1. Open TracknMe and go to Settings.
  2. Scroll down to the Danger Zone.
  3. Tap Wipe Current Sheet Data.
  4. Confirm by tapping Delete Only.

This will permanently delete all records for that specific sheet.

Method 2: Uninstall the App

Simply uninstalling TracknMe from your device using the system settings or home screen will automatically remove all locally stored databases and preferences.

Method 3: Request Purchase Data Deletion

If you made a purchase and want your anonymized purchase history deleted from our payment processor (RevenueCat), please contact us directly using the information below.

Contact Us

For purchase data deletion requests or any questions about your data, contact us.

We will process purchase-related data deletion requests within 30 days.
